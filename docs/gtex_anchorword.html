Comparing with information from GTEx portal¶

Our model is able to find some top genes that are expressed only in one or a few tissue. Yet some top genes have similar expression pattern.

More detailed cmparison below:

(from https://gtexportal.org/home/ and https://github.com/stephenslab/topics/blob/zihao/docs/gtex_loadings_plot_ccd.pdf)

  • TOPIC 12: (proportion of top 10 genes: 0.834395386446502)

    • dominant genes are "mitochondrially encoded"
    • The expression of the most prominant gene are quite similar to the topic loading on that gene

  • TOPIC 18:

    • top genes are almost only expressed in pancreas from GTEx portal, which is exactly what our loading shows for this topic

  • TOPIC 3,5,17,4,8

    • the expression of top gene has very similar pattern, and also only seen in one tissue

  • TOPIC 13:

    • the expression of the top gene is not very similar to the corresponding loadings, especially the expression in brain is much smaller in loading

  • TOPIC 15

    • the expression of top gene is similar to loading in tissues except in cervix (high gene expression which is not observed in corresponding loading)

  • TOPIC 9:

    • high expression of top 2 genes only found in stomach
    • high expression of 5th,9th genes only found in salivary gland
    • expression pattern of 3th, 4th,5th,8th,10th similar (highly expressed in a few and probably similar tissues)
    • expression of 6th and 7th are simialr

  • TOPIC 20

    • top 3 genes are highly expressed in Cells - Transformed fibroblasts (a label not shown in my loading plot)
    • similar to topic 9, many genes are highly expressed in many tissues

  • TOPIC 19

    • top 3 genes highly expressed in pituitary
    • 4th 5th highly expressed in adrenal gland
    • many other genes highly expressed in many other tissues (also similar pattern to many other)

  • TOPIC 6

    • Top 2 genes are highly expressed in skin
    • many other genes highly expressed in many other tissues
  • The rest of the topics:
    • Some have sparse loading, but among the top genes, some express similar to the loading, while some others are highly expressed in many tussues

n_top = 10
topics = {}

for i,x in enumerate(A.T):
    idx = np.argsort(x,)
    top_idx = idx[-n_top:]
    top_idx = top_idx[::-1]
    prop_top = x[top_idx].sum()
    top_genes = genes[top_idx]
    topics[i+1] = {"prop_top": prop_top, "top_genes":top_genes, "proportions":x[top_idx]}

prop_tops_ix = np.argsort([v["prop_top"]  for k,v in topics.items()])
prop_tops_ix = prop_tops_ix[::-1]
for i in prop_tops_ix:
    print("###-----------------------------###\n")
    print("TOPIC {} TOP GENES:\n".format(i+1))
    print("proportion of top {} genes: {}".format(n_top, topics[i+1]["prop_top"]))
    print("gene names\n")
    print(topics[i+1]["top_genes"])
    print("gene proportions")
    print(topics[i+1]["proportions"])
    print("\n")
